## Hello!

Welcome to the Selvi Data Consulting blog. This is where I'll share insights about data engineering, analytics best practices, and interesting visualization techniques.

I work with businesses to help them get more value out of their data — whether that's building pipelines, designing dashboards, or figuring out the right analytics strategy.

## What to expect

Here's what you'll find on this blog:

- **Data engineering** deep dives — pipeline patterns, ETL best practices, and tool comparisons
- **Analytics** guides — metrics design, dashboard patterns, and BI strategies
- **Visualization** showcases — interactive charts, monitoring dashboards, and creative data presentations
