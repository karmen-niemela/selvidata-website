## Work examples

### SaaS platform — scaling past growing pains

A B2B SaaS product was experiencing timeouts as their customer base grew past 10K accounts. I audited their Postgres database, identified missing indexes and N+1 query patterns in their ORM layer, and rewrote critical queries. Average API response time dropped from 1.2s to 90ms.

### Marketplace — data model redesign

A marketplace startup had built their initial schema quickly and it was becoming a bottleneck — circular foreign keys, no audit trail, and denormalized data causing update anomalies. I redesigned the core data model with proper normalization, event sourcing for the transaction layer, and materialized views for read-heavy paths.

### Healthcare startup — compliance-ready database architecture

A health-tech startup needed a database architecture that satisfied HIPAA requirements while remaining performant. I designed a schema with row-level security, encryption at rest, comprehensive audit logging, and a clean separation between PHI and non-sensitive data.
