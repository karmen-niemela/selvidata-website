## What I deliver

- **Data audit** — a clear picture of what data you have, where it lives, and what's missing
- **Tool recommendations** — the right stack for your stage, not the most expensive one
- **Architecture design** — data warehouse, lake, or lakehouse — whatever fits your needs
- **Roadmap** — a phased plan that prioritizes quick wins and builds toward long-term goals
- **Vendor evaluation** — unbiased comparison of tools and platforms for your specific use case

## Work examples

### Seed-stage startup — building from zero

A pre-Series A startup had product data in a production database and marketing data in spreadsheets, with no analytics capability. I conducted a data audit, recommended a lightweight modern stack (Postgres + dbt + Metabase), and delivered a 3-phase roadmap. Phase 1 was live in 4 weeks, giving the founders investor-ready metrics.

### Series B company — consolidating data chaos

A growing company had data spread across 12 different SaaS tools with no single source of truth. I designed a centralized data warehouse architecture using Snowflake, mapped all data sources, and created an integration plan. The team went from "which number is right?" to a single, trusted data platform.

### Enterprise team — modern data stack migration

An established company was running on legacy on-premise infrastructure and wanted to move to the cloud. I evaluated cloud providers, designed a migration strategy with parallel-run validation, and created a phased rollout plan that minimized risk and downtime.
