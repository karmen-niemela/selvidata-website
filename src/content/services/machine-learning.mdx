## Work examples

### Subscription platform — churn prediction

A subscription-based platform wanted to proactively retain users at risk of canceling. I built a gradient-boosted churn prediction model using behavioral and billing data. The model flagged at-risk users 30 days in advance with 86% precision, enabling the customer success team to intervene early and reduce monthly churn by 22%.

### E-commerce — product recommendation engine

An online retailer wanted to personalize their shopping experience beyond simple "customers also bought" logic. I developed a collaborative filtering recommendation system that increased average order value by 15% and improved email campaign click-through rates by 2.3x.

### Logistics company — demand forecasting

A logistics company needed to optimize warehouse staffing based on anticipated order volumes. I built a time-series forecasting model (Prophet + custom features) that predicted daily order volumes 2 weeks out with 91% accuracy, reducing both overtime costs and understaffing incidents.
