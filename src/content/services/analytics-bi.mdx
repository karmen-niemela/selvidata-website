## Work examples

### SaaS startup — first analytics stack

A Series A SaaS company had all their data in a production Postgres database with no reporting layer. I set up a lightweight analytics stack: Fivetran for ingestion, dbt for transformations, and Metabase for dashboards. Within 6 weeks, the exec team had daily KPI dashboards and the product team had self-serve access to user behavior data.

### E-commerce — conversion funnel analysis

An e-commerce brand knew their conversion rate was low but couldn't pinpoint where users were dropping off. I built a full funnel analysis pipeline, instrumenting key events and building cohort-based dashboards. The team identified a checkout friction point that, once fixed, improved conversion by 18%.

### Fintech — investor reporting automation

A fintech startup was spending 2 days each month manually compiling investor reports from spreadsheets. I built an automated reporting pipeline that pulled from their data warehouse and generated formatted reports, cutting the process down to a 15-minute review.
